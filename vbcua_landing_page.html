Three evaluation modes
What VBCUA measures
🧠

Semantic understanding

Compares your explanation against a reference concept using Sentence-BERT embeddings. Surfaces missed ideas, deviations, and conceptual gaps.

SBERT Cosine similarity Concept scoring
🎙️

Fluency analysis

Detects filler words ("um", "uh", "like"), pause ratio, RMS energy, and zero-crossing rate via Librosa to quantify delivery confidence.
